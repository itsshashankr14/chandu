1.BSc

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) is an undergraduate academic degree program that is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in science and mathematics. This program is typically offered in a variety of science-related disciplines, such as Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science, Statistics, and Environmental Science.

  • Eligibility: The eligibility criteria for a B.Sc program may vary depending on the college or university. Generally, candidates must have completed their 10+2 level of education with science subjects as compulsory subjects. Some colleges may also require a minimum percentage in the 10+2 exams.
  • Average Fees: : The average fee for a B.Sc program may vary depending on the college or university and the chosen discipline. The fee can range from INR 20,000 to INR 1 lakh per annum.
  • Duration : The duration of a B.Sc program is typically three years in India.
  • Career Opportunities: : After completing a B.Sc program, graduates can pursue a variety of career paths in industries such as research, education, healthcare, technology, and more. The job opportunities available will depend on the chosen discipline. For example, graduates in Physics can work as research scientists, physicists, or teachers, while graduates in Computer Science can work as software developers, data analysts, or web designers.
